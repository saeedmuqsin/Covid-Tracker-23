How to stop the spread of covid-19

Washing the Hands.

To stop the spread of COVID-19, along with other COVID appropriate behaviours, the practice of handwashing at regular intervals is a must, after coughing or sneezing, when caring for the sick, after using the toilet, before eating, while preparing food and after handling animals or animal waste.

Effective wearing of Face mask

Face masks have been considered a first step to prevent and contain the spread of the disease. Different types of masks are available on the market for this purpose. Simple masks covering mouth and nose are primarily used to prevent transmission by holding back droplets.

Social distancing in gathers.

Why is social distancing important for Covid 19? Limiting our contact with people will slow down virus transmission and flatten the epidemic curve so that we can reduce the number of cases occurring at the peak of the epidemic.
